About me

My name is Maxime Thirouin, also known as MoOx, and I'm a french web developer living in Toulouse, France.

I also like to share everything I learned: I enjoy giving some talks and I'm a teacher in my previous University.

Beside that, I like to enjoy time with the love of my live, create stuff with my hands, take a walk in the forest or play paintball with my friends.
